Privacy Policy

Deal Tracker · Last updated July 10, 2026

Deal Tracker (“the app,” “we”) is an independent tool for sales representatives to scan, log, and track their deals and commissions. This policy explains what the app collects and how it is handled. Deal Tracker is not affiliated with or endorsed by Westgate Resorts or any resort company.

1. Who uses this app

Deal Tracker is used by sales representatives (“you”). In using it, you input information about your customers and deals by photographing sales proposal forms. You are responsible for handling your customers’ information in accordance with your employer’s policies and applicable law.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address and password, used to sign you in.
  • Deal and customer information: when you photograph a proposal form, the app stores the form image and extracts data from it, which may include customer names, phone numbers, purchase and financing terms, market source, and commission details.

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, or your device contacts.

3. How we use information

Solely to provide the app’s functionality: reading deal data from your photos, and showing you your deals, commissions, and related metrics. Each account can access only its own data.

4. Third-party services

The app relies on the following service providers to function:

  • Supabase — hosts the database, authentication, and image storage.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — form images are sent to Anthropic’s API to automatically read and extract the printed fields from the form.

We do not sell your data or your customers’ data, and we do not use it for advertising.

5. Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can delete individual deals in the app, which removes the stored record and its associated image. To delete your account and all associated data, email help@repdealtracker.app.

6. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with access controls so that each account can access only its own records. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Children

The app is intended for professional use by adults and is not directed to children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email help@repdealtracker.app.

Deal Tracker is an independent app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Westgate Resorts or any resort company.
